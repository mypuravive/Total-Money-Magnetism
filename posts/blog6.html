A Short But Powerful Money Hypnosis:

"Allowing yourself to identify with financial abundance, shift your attention now to notice your connection to wealth become much stronger, and then without distraction, until it’s as real as the physical space around you in every way and you feel wealthy like air against your skin."

What is this saying:

  • Encouraging you to immerse yourself in the feeling of financial abundance to the point where it becomes as tangible and real to you as the physical world around you, this passage intends to show that by strengthening your connection to wealth in your mind and emotions, you can experience it as vividly as you feel the air against your skin. In essence, it is an exercise that is meant to help you fully embrace and internalize the feeling of abundance until it becomes an integral part of your reality.

    It does this by guiding you through these steps:

    Identification with Abundance: It moves you to mentally align yourself with the concept of financial abundance. This involves seeing yourself as someone who possesses wealth and prosperity.

    Strengthening the Connection to Wealth: The passage guides you to direct your focus towards strengthening your relationship with wealth. This involves visualizing yourself surrounded by abundance, engaging in activities that evoke feelings of prosperity, and reinforcing positive beliefs about your ability to attract wealth.

    Making Abundance Feel Tangible: It aims to make the feeling of wealth and abundance as real and tangible to you as the physical world around you. By eliminating distractions and focusing your attention solely on the feeling of abundance, you can create a mental state where wealth feels palpable and accessible.

    Feeling Wealthy Like Air Against Your Skin: The passage compares the sensation of feeling wealthy to the experience of feeling air against your skin—it suggests that abundance should become so ingrained in your consciousness that it feels as natural and essential as the air you breathe.

    Overall, the passage guides you through a process of mental and emotional transformation, helping you shift your mindset and perception to one of abundance and prosperity. Through visualization, emotional connection, and embodiment, it aims to make the feeling of financial abundance a deeply ingrained and tangible reality in your life.
