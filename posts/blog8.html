Let's Make you Comfortable With Money

A Short But Powerful Money Hypnosis:

"Thinking about the present moment in any way that brings you a sense of comfort in financial success, begin to notice everything else that increases this feeling for you until you are one with having great financial abundance. Now, relaxing into this further, notice how much more like yourself you feel wealthy, but noticing with your heart."

What is this saying:

  • This statement outlines a mindfulness exercise designed to foster a positive mindset about financial success and abundance. It encourages you to focus on the present moment and identify thoughts or perspectives that make you feel comfortable with the idea of being financially successful. As you immerse yourself in this mindset, you're prompted to notice anything else in your environment or within yourself that reinforces feelings of abundance. The exercise aims to deepen your connection to the concept of financial wealth until you feel fully aligned with it. Ultimately, it encourages you to relax into this mindset and observe how feeling wealthy resonates with your true self, emphasizing the importance of connecting with these feelings on an emotional level.

    By following these steps you can make better use of the passage above:

    Focus on the Present Moment:The exercise starts by prompting you to focus on the present moment. This helps to bring your attention to the here and now, allowing you to let go of distractions and concerns.

    Identify Comforting Thoughts:Next, you're encouraged to identify thoughts or perspectives that make you feel comfortable with the idea of financial success. This could involve thinking about past achievements, envisioning future goals, or simply acknowledging your current blessings.

    Notice Reinforcing Factors:As you immerse yourself in these comforting thoughts, you're prompted to notice anything else in your environment or within yourself that reinforces feelings of abundance. This could include positive aspects of your surroundings, supportive relationships, or personal qualities that contribute to your success.

    Deepen Connection to Abundance:Through continued focus and observation, the exercise aims to deepen your connection to the concept of financial wealth. By immersing yourself in thoughts and sensations related to abundance, you gradually strengthen your belief in your own prosperity.

    Relax into the Mindset:As you become more aligned with feelings of abundance, the exercise encourages you to relax further into this mindset. This involves letting go of any tension or resistance and allowing yourself to fully embrace feelings of wealth and success.

    Connect Emotionally:Finally, the exercise emphasizes the importance of connecting with these feelings on an emotional level. By observing how feeling wealthy resonates with your true self, you reinforce the emotional connection to abundance and solidify your belief in your own prosperity.

    Overall, the exercise uses mindfulness techniques to shift your mindset towards financial success and abundance, helping you cultivate a more positive and empowering relationship with money.
