Let's Make you Comfortable With Money

A Short But Powerful Money Hypnosis:

"Identify a good feeling you associate with having lots of money. Now, with your subconscious mind, make that the dominant feeling you have about money from now on, and then, until it truly is."

What is this saying:

  • This statement is a psychological exercise aimed at altering your mindset about money. It encourages individuals to identify a positive emotion associated with having wealth, then instructs you to make that feeling your predominant association with money through subconscious conditioning. The goal is to cultivate a mindset where the positive feeling about money becomes the dominant perception, potentially influencing one's attitudes and behaviors towards wealth.

    By following these steps you can make better use of the passage above:

    Identifying a Positive Feeling: The first step is to identify a positive emotion or feeling associated with having lots of money. This could be a feeling of security, freedom, abundance, or any other positive emotion that comes to mind when thinking about wealth.

    Subconscious Conditioning: The instruction then involves making this positive feeling the dominant emotion associated with money. This is achieved through subconscious conditioning, which involves influencing the subconscious mind through repeated exposure to specific thoughts or images.

    Repetition and Focus: The key to subconscious conditioning is repetition and focus. By consistently focusing on the identified positive feeling about money, individuals aim to reinforce this association in their subconscious mind over time.

    Visualization: Visualization techniques can be used to enhance the process. This involves mentally picturing scenarios where one experiences abundance and the associated positive emotions. Visualization adds a sensory component to the process, making it more vivid and effective.

    Consistent Practice: Consistency is crucial for subconscious reprogramming. Individuals need to practice this exercise regularly, ideally daily, to reinforce the desired association between money and positive feelings.

    Gradual Shift: Over time, consistent practice gradually shifts one's subconscious beliefs and attitudes towards money. The dominant feeling about money becomes more positive, influencing thoughts, emotions, and behaviors related to wealth.

    By breaking down the process into these steps and understanding how each contributes to subconscious conditioning, individuals can effectively apply this technique to cultivate a more positive mindset about money.
