Understanding Mindfulness:

At its core, mindfulness is the practice of paying attention to the present moment without judgment. It involves becoming aware of our thoughts, emotions, and bodily sensations with openness and curiosity. Through mindfulness, we learn to observe our inner experiences without getting swept away by them, fostering a greater sense of clarity and inner peace.
